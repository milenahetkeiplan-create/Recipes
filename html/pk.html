Pancakes

This makes about 20 pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 200g flour
  • 2 TL baking powder
  • 1/4 TL salt
  • 1 1/2 TL sugar
  • 1/2 package vanillesugar
  • 2 dl milk
  • 2 eggs

Method

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, salt, sugar and vanillesugar. Create a hollow and pour the liquids and the eggs in it. Mix everything till it's an even dough.
  2. Heat butter in a frying pan. Pour dough into the pan so that you have pancakes around 8 cm Ã˜. With middle heat bake both sides goldbrown and put them somewhere warm till all of them are done.

I recomend eating the pancakes with maple syrup.
